Nintendo Adventure Books 12: Brain Drain map


Intro: Book by Matt Wayne and Archway Paperback. In the twelve Adventure Book Iggy swaps the brains of those in the Mushroom Kingdom but perhaps a certain popular soda is the key to it all! This is an online adaptation of a guide I hand wrote around 5/9/19. To follow along F3 search the page listed (type out ‘page x’) or simply scroll down (or occasionally up). It’s not perfect but this worked the best for me. Look at the key page on the portal for the terminology that will make up this walk through and all others.

Summary: A favorite as a kid and a fun book overall. Some of the body switching antics gave me laughs even if it didn’t go as far as it could have. I like that it was based on Super Mario World but not overtly an advertisement like Dinosaur Dilemma. I also like getting a look into Koopa HQ with Luigi as the koopa. The story structure is fine, it’s not too open ended or linear. There aren’t really any loop back and reused pages. You are always guided towards the end but the book really does allow different ways to get to the end goal as long as you get the one needed item at the start of course. Other parts of the book suffer unfortunately. The mystery is obvious quickly and several puzzles are of the ‘pick a path’ sort with no definitive correct answer. Along with that there are a few errors and puzzles designed sort of badly or way more work than they need to be. I wouldn’t call this book to malicious overall as there are not trap items of doom though the character Dr. Shrinkasaurus always means trouble when he shows so don’t listen to him! Unlike my other guides the optimal path here will attempt a score while still being quick and direct. Flaws are the puzzle design, a few dumb moments in-story, and a crucial item sort of buried at the start and not hinted at all with zero options to obtain it again. 4/5


Optimal path: START 1- 40- 96 (50pts)- 43- 55 (tape)- 29 – 82 (establish secret oath) – 119- 19( 70pts*) – 109- 51 (50pts)- 33 (50pts)- 49 (50pts)- 87- 103- 102- 64 (50pts, pizza)- 5- 112 (100 pts)- 10- 7 - 26 (100 pts) -78- 62 (100pts) -107- 17- 46- 36 END


So this route uses one in a half items, the tape to play at WMUSH and the oath which is not credited on the score card. We never get the wrench either but absolutely get that tape. The pizza is gained only for the points as we avoid the path that requires Luigi to actually plug his ears. That path isn’t bad but skipping it is shorter. How here’s an interesting thing. On page nineteen you need lesser points to go to page one-hundred nine which is great because I kept trying for a while and couldn’t get high enough for the other path. I don’t have it listed as an error because it could just be me. The code you need for the subliminal message puzzle can theoretically be skipped and the book sort of mentions that you might have missed the hint. Even if you miss it you have a fifty shot at guessing which isn’t really that bad. Alternate paths can be taken here or there which reflects well on the book overall.


Score: 620, not the highest but I’m not sure how high you could go because you’re forced to not score well at one point for a certain path. Of course, as mentioned earlier I couldn’t max more than seventy points anyway.
