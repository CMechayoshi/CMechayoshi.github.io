Nintendo Adventure Books 12: Brain Drain map


Intro: Book by Matt Wayne and Archway Paperback. In the twelve Adventure Book Iggy swaps the brains of those in the Mushroom Kingdom but perhaps a certain popular soda is the key to it all! This is an online adaptation of a guide I hand wrote around 5/8/19. To follow along F3 search the page listed (type out ‘page x’) or simply scroll down (or occasionally up). It’s not perfect but this worked the best for me. Look at the key page on the portal for the terminology that will make up this walk through and all others.


Relevant links: scan of score card can be found here. https://vovatia.wordpress.com/2014/12/19/nintendo-in-book-form/

Statistics:

  • Sixty-three chapters

  • Thirteen endings

  • Errors: The fourth paper item is never used. No ‘Mega Mole’ layer. You don’t lose your items to gain them back as a page suggests.

  • Items: Wrench, tape (cassette), paper (plumber’s oath?), pizza





START Chapter 1 Page 1: The Mario bros are working on a large messing and problematic soda machine in the castle. Meanwhile the radio blasts an advert on ‘Fungus Up’ before making horrible noises. Use the puzzle to know what to do .

Puzzle: 1

Splits: page 40, 100

Puzzle solution: A little confusing at first but imagine the letters are on the squares then use the key to navigate. You can land anywhere though at this point and be okay but the optimal path (see far below) says head to forty from points or an item. Path one-hundred offers nothing so avoid it.


Page 40 Chapter 19: Luigi will deal with the potential flood first but what tool to use?

Spits: page 61, 96

Path: from page 1

Puzzle solution: sixty-one gives the item but the other points. If you play your cards right you wont need the item and if you see the optimal path they isn’t a ton of places to reliably get points.


Page 61 Chapter 50: Luigi returns with the wrench but Mario commands him to skip it and head for the radio. Oh well! When he does so Yoshi has an unexpected quip.

Reward: wrench

Splits: page 43

Path: from page 40


Page 96 Chapter 50: Luigi passes the spanner to Mario then fixes the radio badly so Yoshi has a surprise remark.

Reward: fifty points

Splits: page 43

Path: from page 40


Page 100 Chapter 53: Luigi tries the radio first and Yoshi spits something rude.

Splits: 43

Path: from page 1.


Page 43 Chapter 22: Yoshi is clearly not Yoshi anymore. Luigi still wants his dang soda though. Use the puzzle to know what to prioritize.

Puzzle: 1

Splits: page 71, 55

Path: from page 61, 96, 100

Puzzle solution: You are given directions involving the wall of letters and shapes. It will say ‘the radio’ as if it wasn’t already obvious what’s switching brains. Both paths are safe for now but you need that tape item for the end so head to fifty-five for a late payoff.


Page 71 Chapter 38: They find out that others brains have been switched like Toad and the King. At loss for what to do, should Luigi switch on the radio?

Puzzle: 1

Splits: page 32, 76

Path: from page 43

Puzzle solution: The ‘puzzle’ is actually a little recap quiz of book events so far, cute enough. Add up the right answers to get the ‘off’ option on page seventy-six.


Page 32 Chapter 15: Luigi gets switches with a hamster in an exercise wheel.

Game over

Path: from page 71


Page 76 Chapter 41: So everyone correctly deduces the koopas are behind it all. After antics they get going.

Reward: fifty points

Splits: page 29

Path: from page 71


Page 55 Chapter 27: Luigi wants his soda first and finds his old cassette too. They learn of the brain swap between Toad and the King.

Puzzle: 1

Reward: tape item

Splits: page 29

Path: from page 43

Puzzle solution: First solve the math puzzle, you fill find that there are two ounces of Fungus Up. Use that to count backwards from the letters in the message. It says ‘Stops brain switch’.
