Mario and Bowser: Frenemies Forever/ New Frenemy Adventure/ Redux

"Mario and Bowser: Frenemies Forever": (Live FF.net link) You know what they say, keep your friends close and your enemies closer! Will Mario and Bowser's associates approve? Was the illness that disrupted their tennis match part of something bigger?

My signature project from early 2016 through late 2017, my second submission to FF.net, and my first work written specifically for the site. Loosely inspired by an old offline handwritten comic of mine called 'J-Man and the Dragon' and EchoEternal's 'Eternally Never Yours' (or specifically the first few chapters of that), this story was dedicated to Mario and Bowser's intense rivalry and camaraderie. While rocky it is one of my most popular stories. The hyperlink is preserved above, but below you can find it without ads or leaving the site.

  • Archive: Chapters 1-13, Chapters 14-28, Unused snippets, previously shared on tumblr here!
Click for more of my observations (may contain spoilers)
  • I regard this fic better now than I did after completion in 2017, where all I could notice were the errors. I can appreciate this today as an off the cuff yet earnest effort. As expounded in supplementary material like the Tropes doc, the real plot here involves how Mario and Bowser's friends react to the stunning revelation that they are 'friends', while the titular duo get separated after a few chapters. Not technically a mistake, just a consequence of me making this up as I went. I do try to make characters dynamic and interesting and I believe I succeed on some level, despite this being my first time writing most of them. It's not trying to hard to be funny nor get over its head with a cosmic conflict. (See the spoilers of the next story). If I could tighten up parts and cut the chatter, I'd rate this just behind Redux. When FF1 gains momentum I think it's a great story with a bookend-like conclusion that is far more natural than it's sequels. The two epilogues are clunkily me trying to patch up loose ends of course, but ultimately ambiguity remains over certain bits, the better choice compared to again some observations you'll see below.

Mario and Bowser's New Frenemy Adventure

Mario and friends (and enemies) experience love, hate, and everything in between as they learn that sometimes the true villains aren't the ones fought in the games... Other Genres: Humor/ Suspense (T rating for later chapters)

A direct response to some of what I considered to be the largest issues of Frenemies Forever, including the fact that Mario and Bowser don't get to work together for long. The first few chapters are a recreation of 'FF1', but then spirals considerably with a newly introduced foe that cannot be defeated by mere mortals! The stakes are always rising in this one, and there are more character interactions and jokes than ever. It was completed entirely in the year of 2018 and so planned in advance that I have a plethora of unused material and alternate directions linked below. Notably it also signified a shift into more mature themes with and more T rated content. There's some cool moments all throughout the story, but if you're not already a fan of the series, I'd recommend you stick with FF1 to see my rough draft of the idea and Redux for the finalized. See the spoilers for the straight dope.

Archive: Chapters 1-11 and Chapters 12-23 here.

Click for more of my observations (may contain spoilers)
  • I grew a lot from this project, what I learned for a certainty making my later works much more focused and restrained. For 'NFA' I struggled with ambivalence on direction and it shows. The end result is coherent, but from being cobbled from various ideas meant to be more than they are, it's not balanced. There is a plot, and unlike FF1 things don't just randomly get introduced, but antic filled sequences take the forefront often. Returning characters were exaggerated to various degrees for the sake of comedy. At the same time when trying to flesh them out, I did so via some slower interlude like moments to mixed feedback, plus sometimes we were doing that with OCs, which will also be YMMW. There are still a lot of cool things going on in this fic, but there for the sake of being there. It's like a fan fic of a fan fic, not the easiest sale when 'FF2' and Redux put their story first and squeeze the funny bits, antics, and banter in between the gaps left.

Mario and Bowser: Frenemies Forever (Redux)

Hosted on FF.net and A03, from 2022-23. Rewritten late 2024. This replaced Frenemies Forever 2 on the internet (except for my archive) and is my final take:

"As the adage goes, 'keep your friends close and your enemies closer! -What if the enemy was too close? When the Mushroom Kingdom to Dark Land Peace Pact is burnt up and scattered into the wind by an elliptical threat, everyone's secrets, even those buried deepest must come out.

Taking heavier cues from 'FF1', Redux is a tale secrets, conspiracies, different experiences in life, and how people deal and have dealt with it. Oh and there's a legion of super villains out there, ready to destroy Earth, so they'd better get it together soon.

Read: Chapters 1-14. Chapters 15-26

Click for more of my observations (global), (may contain spoilers)
  • The original 2016 Frenemies story was created during a very uncertain and transitional period in life, manifesting in a tale that shifts from jokey to serious to me just doing my best. For New Frenemy Adventure in 2018, life was more stable and it showed with me being creative and experimental, unable to reel in what kind of tale I wanted to make. The saccharine conclusion and exaggerated characters striped some of the depth their older FF1 versions had, so I made Frenemies 2. Written initially in 2019, was to reign it in big time, to try to be raw, even bittersweet. In execution however, I felt like I'd hammered in the wrong message, with a conclusion that's not satisfying enough. That lead to Redux in 2022.
  • The aim for Redux was to take what I felt worked and drop what didn't. don't overly shy away from references, new characters, etc, yet I make sure whatever happens is primarily to have plot relevance. The tone is finally consistent from the first to final chapter. The rewrite and later 2025 edits adjusts and enhances elements better explained in supplementary material # 2 above, or under 'fixer fic' on the Trope page, but does not fundamentally change what the story is. Check it out!

Peach the Punk Princess

Not pictured: graffiti, alcohol, riots..

Summary: What if Peach was Punk? How would her friends and family react? Loosely inspired by Super Mario Bros Comic: Magic Carpet Madness. (Note, not really based on that comic at all aside from how Peach looks on the cover.)

A simple AU idea, so much so that I undersell the plot. Peach is a punky aristocrat who wants to have fun, but will sneaking to a concert at Bowser's Castle get her in a world of trouble? The development was off and on, staring mid 2017 and finishing early 2019, but there was a lot of passion behind this wild trip.

Chapters 1-7

If you look at the date for that chapter, it is BEFORE 'Mario and Bowser's New Frenemy Adventure', which explains quite a bit. Bigger stories were usually where I refined characterization. Last, I defictionalized 'The Mario Clash' from this story. Other than that *chef's kiss*

Chemistry

That moment when you create a baseball team and ten percent (maybe more depending on Diddy, Dixie, and Tiny) aren't of legal drinking age..

Summary: In the world of Mario, why does THIS character and THAT character hate each other? A series of snarky fandom and meta commentary on silly roster choices and/or rivalries, memes, and more in the Mario universe. Starring: Everyone!… eventually...just give it time!

While this starts out as Mario Superstar Baseball story of all things, it soon devolves a loose plot that's more or less a vehicle for me to be snarky about the contemporary Mario fandom. Logically, this wouldn't age well forever. We have baby character bashing, Bowsette bashing, plus other memes mentioned with some Frenemies Forever OCs randomly here or there. Some draft versions of the first chapter were posted on my Tumblr back in the day, but no reason to link those. (Except I did later anyway; see here) It's not that interesting. Lastly, the last line in the summary was supposed to be a reference to Magikoopa981's 'Nuclear Phantasy'. See the spoilers for the straight dope.

Chapters 1-8

Click for more of my observations (may contain spoilers)

It's so specific sometimes that I understand why a lot of readers won't get it, and the plot is basically a string of antics so I could target whatever I wanted, but whatever. Really that explained it. We sorta have another 'Chemistry' here, full of snark in a more organized fashion. The continuity is of New Frenemy Adventure, and it's in first person from Toadette's pov. I'm proud of this prompt to this day. Read the legitimately counter culture one-shot here.

Click for more of my observations (may contain spoilers)

Rhapsody est Vendere/The Day Ludwig Went Pop

Art by wackko200, whom I will gladly shoutout, especially since I think she was a contributor of Lemmy's Land.

Summaries: Ludwig Von Koopa, master composer is making ridiculously dumb pop music? Is the greatest musician in Darkland now a sell out? Now how will he recover from his damaged reputation? -AND- For the master composer, Ludwig Von Koopa, concerts are a way of life. But what about pop concerts? How will his friends and family take it? For music lovers!

Yes, I love Ludwig and music. The Rhapsody story, loosely translating to 'Rhapsody of a Sell-out' was an early submission, with it's attempts at humor making a sensitive reviewer pop off. That was baffling, unexpected, and sorta funny at the time, but not where I really want to go with the concept of Ludwig going 'pop'.

I created in 2017 the second story, a more affectionate take and a one shot that wraps itself up nicely. Check out where it began in Rhapsody Est Vendere here. (Three chapters) Or the later, more refined Ludwig goes Pop here. (One shot).

Click for more of my observations (may contain spoilers)
  • Rhapsody is all over the place, very contemporary of Frenemies Forever 1 by being speech centric and about conspiracies. The characterization is off for some Koopalings, but this was actually the first fan fic I'd done with them, not counting Slammerversestuff. Note that despite pop and dubstep and other popular genres were being jabbed at here, this clearly isn't me slamming what I hate, though while demonstrating Iggy's insanity (another character trait I'd drop in other tales) I made fun of 'triggering' which appropriately triggered some guest reviewer. Quite an overreaction if you see the actual 'joke'.
  • Back to square one with Ludwig Goes Pop, with a conflict that's more internal than external, though Ludwig still faces some ribbing over his appreciation of less sophisticated genres. I put in extra backstory, toss in a few minor OCs to complete the picture and give it a happy ending. The only thing I'd change would be to not reference real songs and singers, just a pet peeve of mine. This being 2017 written though, that predates New Frenemy Adventure where I'd start parodying names. Anyway, enjoy!
