Super Mario Advance: Choose Your Own Adventure Guide




Dis: This is a walk-through for the SMA: CYOA book. This will work similar to how I create guides on the Nintendo Adventure Books but simplified. I will start with the page number and the page’s description from the book. A ‘split’ is where a page leads, a ‘path’ are pages that lead to that page. This book has an intro, a character split, and the party section. This how I will show the paths. Occasionally I use use a ‘Puzzle Solution’ part to reveal something of interest. Of course there aren’t any actual puzzles..
