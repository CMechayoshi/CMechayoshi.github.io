Peach the Punk Princess:

Too bad the cover doesn't show her punk getup

-This story has a simple AU ideal. 'What if Peach was Punk?'. The development was long, staring mid 2017 and finishing early 2019, but there was a lot of passion behind it, because I'm into music. My project 'The Mario Clash' came from this story for instance. It's a short but wild trip for Peach with a few references to other works of mine.

Summary: What if Peach was Punk? How would her friends and family react? Loosely inspired by Super Mario Bros Comic: Magic Carpet Madness. (Note, not really based on that comic at all aside from how peach looks on the cover.)

Monster Mix-Up

Doesn't perfectly match but close enough..

Summary: Based off of the Nintendo Adventure Book, Bowser is making mega-monsters and its up to Mario and friends to stop him! Long before creating my Nintendo Adventure Books guides, I did a pseudo version of that with this early fan-fiction. It's a novelization primarily of the infamous to my childhood Book #3 Monster Mixup, but since I have Mario make all of the correct choices, and in later chapters have author notes telling you what's up, it functions as a walkthrough too.

Other fun facts, this was the only story I started in 2016, the year I began to upload to FF.net and completed in that same year. Others would have to get their conclusion in 2017 or later, including Frenemies Forever org. I made a bizarre remix there here. Summary: A corruption of the Nintendo Adventure Book and Pokemon Vietnamese Crystal. Bowser is making mega monsters...in bootleg zone!

Summary: In the world of Mario, why does THIS character and THAT character hate each other? Mr. Game & Watch Does a Thing

Pictured: One thing he's not allowed to do these days.

Summary: Every Smash bros fanfiction in one story. Also Mr. G&W will do a thing, of course. Inspired by: Yoshizilla of course. Plus a few other fanfics I've read. Can you spot the references? This one, like Chemistry is tied topically to a certain era, even more meta in a way because I'm playing with tropes and setups of popular Smash Brothers fan fictions in this story. While the short quirky style is a ref to Yoshizilla Rhedosaurus (who favorited this and noticed Senpai!), another big one was Smash Brothers on Facebook, Hot Topic, and other meme stories, plus some very early Frenemies Forever stuff tossed in with OCs. I write in general a couple of characters you'll never see in other works of mine, as they aren't Mario ones. As this is purely in 2017, it's super sophomoric and never meant to be taken too seriously, so enjoy. -Unfinished unfortunately.

Psyche Out!

Left: What I titled this strange adventure after. Right: Where I went with the idea. Well.. sorta..

Summary: One innocent Monday Mario and friends realize they now possess a new 'gift'- All except for one of them. How does his world cope when secrets can be no more? Based on a writing prompt and part of a series of Mario stories I was doing in 2018. Soon to be added here, they consisted of ideals I'd never seen in a story. Here Mario and co try to live their lives, again in a world vaguely from Frenemies Forever, except they can read each other's mind. Well, almost all of them, leading to the twists and turns in store. Besides being a one shot, very atypical for me, its a Marioish save the princess plot, another thing I don't like to do normally. This never gained a ton of attention but liked the ideal and execution, and this was actually written out on paper before digital upload. I speak about it in author notes. -One shot.

Goombas were the last to join Bowser's army

THIS GUY! THIS GUY RIGHT HERE!... Oh this isn't Youtube? I don't have to point?...

Summary: Goombas were the last to join Bowser's army. (Fact) This however isn't. (Maybe) Part of my 'experimental prompt' series sorta for 2018, this one-shot is based on the fact that goombas were the last enemy programmed for Super Mario Bros 1. Why? Koopa Troopas were thought to be a little too advanced for a brand new player, so the goombas that greet you first in this famous game were the last! Irony! This also creates, as I touch on in story, the narrative that the goombas were once with the Mushroom Kingdom but rebelled, an angle actual games after SMB1 don't touch on ever. Enjoy this short little story.

Toad in the Whoa Zone

Story events are far far stranger than whatever's going on here..

Summary: Can Toad keep his head straight as he discovers new and weird alternate universes? A very odd one of mine and one of the longest lasting stories, created late 2016 and actually completed early 2019. And yet it's only four chapters! Firstly, not this isn't based on Super Paper Mario. It might have went there at some point, but like many early stories this was seat of pants. What's a little unique is that I referenced this not once, but twice in the original Frenemies Forever (And Monster Mix up too in those). The short tale we have is surprisingly written well beyond the first chapter of antics and chatter, plus I find it funny, even though this was more about me being experimental and provocative than 'comedic' (compared to Chemistry for instance). Enjoy something different.
