Peach the Punk Princess:

Too bad the cover doesn't show her punk getup

-This story has a simple AU ideal. 'What if Peach was Punk?'. The development was long, staring mid 2017 and finishing early 2019, but there was a lot of passion behind it, because I'm into music. My project 'The Mario Clash' came from this story for instance. It's a short but wild trip for Peach with a few references to other works of mine.

Summary: What if Peach was Punk? How would her friends and family react? Loosely inspired by Super Mario Bros Comic: Magic Carpet Madness. (Note, not really based on that comic at all aside from how peach looks on the cover.)

Monster Mix-Up

Doesn't perfectly match but close enough..

Summary: Based off of the Nintendo Adventure Book, Bowser is making mega-monsters and its up to Mario and friends to stop him! Long before creating my Nintendo Adventure Books guides, I did a pseudo version of that with this early fan-fiction. It's a novelization primarily of the infamous to my childhood Book #3 Monster Mixup, but since I have Mario make all of the correct choices, and in later chapters have author notes telling you what's up, it functions as a walkthrough too.

Other fun facts, this was the only story I started in 2016, the year I began to upload to FF.net and completed in that same year. Others would have to get their conclusion in 2017 or later, including Frenemies Forever org. I made a bizarre remix there here. Summary: A corruption of the Nintendo Adventure Book and Pokemon Vietnamese Crystal. Bowser is making mega monsters...in bootleg zone!

A Wolf in Mario's Clothing

What? You thought this was a Paper Mario game? Psyche!

Summary: Based off of Paper Mario: TTYD. Doopliss steals Mario's identity and has some fun. He's gonna be one 'Super' Mario...(Previously named 'A Freak in Mario's Clothing') Okay, the images are getting less and less related I know, but this was a cool ideal I hadn't seen anyone do then or now. It's based on the short stunt of Doopliss copying Mario and that's it. Now it's for sure and old story, and there's a few self admitted errors, see author notes, but it's cool over all. I was proud of it, and it features characters I don't usually write, because I don't usually novelize games.
