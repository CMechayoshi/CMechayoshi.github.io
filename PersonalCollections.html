Lemmy's Land.. tribute.. thing..

Credits to wackko200 or Latisha Banks as I just learned, where a lot of the iconic art came from.

This was a Mario fan site thriving in the early 00s when I found it. Like how many others feel, it was super fun to waste time there reading the fan-fiction (or fun fiction and scribbles as was their terminology), play games, look at the art, checkout the contests, reviews, even dip into the forums. I was 'super_stanly' there, a reference to Stanley the Bugman. I was aware of that character due to the Neglected Mario Character sprite comics, but that's another tale for another day. The review section validated my hate at the time of Super Mario Sunshine, yes I thought it was overrated way back when compared to Mario 64 and thought I was the only one with issues with it. I also tried to mess around on the forums, caused some hoopla somehow with something I can't remember saying, and gave up there, going to Nintendoland instead. Let's not talk about that either!

Unfortunately this was years before I started to submit fan-fiction online so I am memorialized there only with four little submissions. Was I destined a reviewer, complete with the shilling and scandals? Thankfully not to be honest, but here they are anyway, neat little look into by perspectives back when I was starting to get into collecting retro games. Webmaster 'Lemmy' would personally email you and give pointers, as he commented on my improvement in submissions. I still have some of his correspondence printed out. Dated 5/1/ 2009 that means I was quite illiterate as a teen! Actually my typing proficiency was lacking and I would get lazy. With slow internet until the next year, I couldn't participate much online and had little incentive to type often. I fixed that quickly. Anyway, check it out!
