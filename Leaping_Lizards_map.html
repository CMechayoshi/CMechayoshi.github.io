Nintendo Adventure Books 3: Monster Mix-Up map


Intro: Book by Clyde Bosco and Archway Paperback. This adventure stars Luigi who must save the King that has been transformed into a rabbit and eventually compete in some epic games. This guide is adapted from a handwritten draft I completed on 4/29/19 using my own physical copy of this book but I will provide a digital link below. This ‘map’ or guide will cover every page in the book but it’s set up as if you are reading the book so it jumps when the book jumps. To follow along F3 search the page listed (type out ‘page x’) or simply scroll down (or occasionally up). It’s not perfect but this worked the best for me. Look at the key page on the portal for the terminology that will make up this walk through and all others.

Statistics:

  • Fifty-eight chapters

  • Six(?) endings

  • Errors: Page fifty-five’s puzzle. Puzzles like page seventy-nine or one-hundred eleven are also a little messed up or can’t be entirely completed. Much more minor, but a page’s image is incorrectly showing the wrong character once.

  • Items: Anchor, wand, wings, frog suit, pogo stick





START Chapter 1 Page 1: Luigi visits the Mushroom Kingdom, racing around for fun. He hid away supplies last trip so use the opening puzzle to determine what he finds.

Puzzle: 1

Rewards: anchor or frog suit, or wings

Splits: page 5

Puzzle solution: Follow the maze to see how many wrenches you get. Seven is the anchor, five or six frog suit, and four the wings. Get the frog suit according to the optimal route listed at the bottom of the guide or at the very least DON’T get the anchor!



Page 5 Chapter 2: Luigi meets friends at a picnic. Soon Toad rushes up with urgent news.

Reward: Five coins

Splits: page 64

Path: from page 1



Page 64 Chapter 29: Toad informs them that Morton is making a mess of the palace. Mario wants to head there but Luigi thinks he’s faster. Use the puzzle to know which brother to send.

Puzzle: 1

Splits: page 45, page 27

Path: from page 5

Puzzle solution: Color in the triangle shapes and it will spell out ‘send Luigi’ so head to twenty-seven.



Page 27 Chapter 12: Luigi dashes to the palace first. There is a mess, people are missing, etc, except for Morton Koopa who should be there obviously. Chase him using this puzzle.

Puzzle: 1

Splits: page 98, page 78

Path: from page 64

Puzzle solution: You follow a path to land on Morton or the window. Both can work at this point but landing on Morton will give you the item right away.



Page 98 Chapter 56: Luigi tackles Morton. The wand is dropped and Luigi gets to it first. Morton escapes as Mario and co arrive finally. Go to thirty-seven.

Reward: three coins, wand

Splits: page 37

Path: from page 27



Page 78 Chapter 36: Luigi dives but misses just as Mario and co arrive. The Princess is now upset and for good reason. Go to thirty-seven.

Splits: page 37

Path: from page 98



Page 45 Chapter 20: In this path the entire group travels together to the palace. Gerkins and Brock (only named on this path), the guards are asleep and the place is a mess. No one is around mostly including Morton so the Princess blows up before meeting Wooster.

Splits: page 37

Path: from page 64



Page 37 Chapter 16: Wooster tells the bad news involving the King’s transformation into a small fluffy white rabbit. Luigi volunteers himself to act.

Splits: page 69, page 110

Path: from page 98, page 78, page 45

Puzzle solution: Use the wand if you have it but if not the other path is safe as long as you lack the anchor item from the very start.



Page 69 Chapter 32: Luigi tires to use the ruby wand but he must choose the right word. Use the puzzle to know.

Puzzle: 1

Splits: page 18, page 104

Path: from page 37, page 91

Puzzle solution: Just follow the maze and it will be ‘alakazook’ on page one-hundred four. The page eighteen option is a hazard.



Page 18 Chapter 8: Luigi makes his coins transform into rabbit hair. He tries the correct word next.

Buffet: lose five coins

Splits: page 104



Page 104 Chapter 50: Luigi turns the King into a bigger rabbit. Now he must decided to try the wand again or go after Morton.

Splits: page 73, page 119

Path: from page 69

Puzzle Solution: Head to seventy-three for Morton.



Page 119 Chapter 58: Luigi make the King larger than the room they’re in and it gets worse from there..

Game over

Path: from page 104



Page 110 Chapter 53: Luigi is chasing Morton when the Koopaling jumps in a boat. How he gives chase depends on if he has an item.

Splits: page 50, page 76

Path: from page 37

Puzzle solution: Don’t attempt this path if you have the anchor!



Page 50 Chapter 22: Luigi swims but is weighed down by the anchor and sucked into a pipe leading to Pipe Land. No harm occurs, but he going to have the miss the adventure so same thing?..

Game over

Path: page 110, page 12



Page 76 Chapter 35: Luigi swims after Morton’s canoe and is ready to pounce. Use the puzzle carefully.

Puzzle: 1

Splits: page 94, page 91

Path: from page 110

Puzzle solution: You trace a path that will either miss or hit the boat. You want to MISS on page ninety-one.




Page 94 Chapter 43: Morton whacks Luigi with the paddle, knocking him out. Morton ponders if Luigi’s cap covered in ‘koopa klay’ will be a good ashtray (yes it says that). The page’s image mistakenly shows Roy.

Game over

Path: from page 76



Page 91 Chapter 42: Luigi rocks Morton’s boat then tossed the koopaling on shore. He gets the wand and then head to the palace. The puzzle reveals a quirk about the ruby wand.

Puzzle: 1

Reward: seven coins, wand

Split: page 69

Path: from page 76

Puzzle solution: Follow the rules to cross out certain wands. The message ‘it’s a one way wand’ will be revealed, a hint that the other path to page sixty-nine curiously omits.



Page 73 Chapter 34: Luigi and Toad head off to find Morton, being lead to Water Land. The puzzle provides a preview of what’s to come.

Puzzle: 1

Split: page 117

Path: from page 104

Puzzle solution: This requires mirroring the image then marking off letters in the water. It will mention a ‘doom ship’. Now move on.



Page 117 Chapter 57: In Water Land, Luigi and Toad face cheep cheeps before Toad has enough. Luigi now travels alone before finding signs of Koopa activity.

Reward: six coins

Split: page 58

Path: from page 73



Page 58 Chapter 27: Luigi investigates the houseboat before Morton spots him. The koopaling is worn out so she calls a doom ship for an escape. Iggy however betrays Morton slightly by pulling the ladder up too soon (also Iggy is younger than Morton here). Anyway, what should Luigi go after?

Puzzle: 1

Splits: page 62, page 7

Path: 117

Puzzle solution: Swap the odd numbered letters but it visualize this properly you might need to rewrite the letters on a separate piece of paper then swap letters reading from bottom to top. The two messages are ‘Go after iggy now’ and ‘Morton cant help’. So go to page sixty-two accordingly for the optimal route but the other one is safe.



Page 62 Chapter 28: Luigi boards the doomship. Should he pounce or hold steady? Use the puzzle.

Puzzle: 1

Split: page 100, page 114

Path: from page 58

Puzzle solution: An easy puzzle that spells ‘do not move’. So don’t!



Page 7 Chapter 3: Luigi swims after Morton and captures him rather impressively. Exhausted, Morton finally has some answers, or so it seems.

Split: page 79

Path: from page 58, page 12, page 67



Page 79 Chapter 37: Morton tells Luigi of the sliver whistle Iggy has. Luigi then lets him go when Mario and co on a flying carpet find him. They take off and spot the doom ship getting away. The puzzle reveals the location.

Puzzle: 1 (error?)

Split: page 87

Path: from page 7

Puzzle solution: The instructions are easy but the results other than the first phrase being ‘Iggy’ are unclear. I have this marked as an error.



Page 87 Chapter 40: The heroes take the carpet underground. They explore the tunnels before finding Iggy. Go to page eight.

Split: page 8

Path: from page 79



Page 100 Chapter 47: Luigi steps from behind the crate to confront Iggy. Iggy does a daring move to throw him off, literally.

Split: page 12

Path: from page 62

Puzzle solution: Part of a small loop back. Just keep moving.


Page 12 Chapter 5: Luigi falls in Water Land.

Splits: page 50, page 7

Path: from page 100, page 114

Puzzle solution: You don’t have the anchor hopefully so you should be able to take page seven.
